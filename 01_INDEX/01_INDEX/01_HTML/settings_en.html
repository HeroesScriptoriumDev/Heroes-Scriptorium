Billing & Subscription

The Billing System:

There was an idea. To bring together a group of legacy players
who have often felt left behind and forgotten by modern platforms,
neglected in their own community. Heroes Scriptorium is the response
to that idea. It was built on one simple principle: access to the game
should not depend on a subscription.


The entirty of the apps functions, from character creation, campaigns,
forums, community posts, and future tools will remain available without
mandatory payments. If a payment system is ever introduced, it will be voluntary
and will never be a means to gatekeep access to the platform's
core features and designs.
